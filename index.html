About The Symposium

GILBERTRONIX'2K25 is a premier National Level Technical Symposium by the Dept. of EEE at P.S.R. Engineering College. It's a dynamic platform for students to showcase technical prowess, engage in stimulating competitions, and connect with peers from across the nation. Join us for a day of innovation, learning, and celebration.
